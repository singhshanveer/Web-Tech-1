Welcome to Delicious

Delivering great food for more than 10 years!

Opened in the summer of 2013 in Toronto, by Paul Smith,

Delicious soon became a popular place for breakfast, lunch, all-day brunch and pastries.

The Toronto location opened in December 2013. It has a very similar warm atmosphere: lots of wood, lots of plants. The culinary concept follows the footsteps of its Delicious cafe and has numerous Japanese influences: comfort food with thoughtfully sourced, local ingredients freshly prepared with Japanese flavors whenever possible. Homemade bread, croissants and pastries are all freshly baked throughout the day. In February 2019, the Delicious cafe opened its doors for dinner unveiling original cocktails and a savory evening menu.
